Adam B. Schiff is a Democratic U.S. Senator from California, serving since January 3, 2025. He previously served as a U.S. Representative for California's 28th/30th congressional district from 2001 to 2025, including as a leading figure on national security and foreign policy and as the ranking member and chair of the House Intelligence Committee. Schiff is known for his work on oversight of the executive branch, national security matters, and foreign policy.
