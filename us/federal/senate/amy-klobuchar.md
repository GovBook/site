Amy Jean Klobuchar is a United States Senator from Minnesota, first elected in 2006 and taking office in 2007. A member of the Democratic Party, she has focused on issues including antitrust enforcement, healthcare, infrastructure, and elections reform. She has chaired the Senate Democratic Steering Committee since 2019.
