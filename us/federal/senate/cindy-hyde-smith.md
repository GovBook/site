Cindy Hyde-Smith is the junior United States Senator from Mississippi, serving since 2018. She was appointed to the seat to fill the vacancy created by the retirement of Thad Cochran and subsequently won reelection. She previously served as Mississippi Commissioner of Agriculture and Commerce.
