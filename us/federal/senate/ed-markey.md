Edward J. Markey is the Democratic United States Senator from Massachusetts, serving since January 3, 2013, after representing Massachusetts' 7th congressional district in the U.S. House from 1975 to 2013. He is a longtime advocate for climate action, energy policy, health care, and consumer protection.
