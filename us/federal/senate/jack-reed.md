John F. Reed, commonly known as Jack Reed, is a United States Senator from Rhode Island, serving since 1997. He is a member of the Democratic Party and has been a leading voice on national security, veterans' affairs, and fiscal policy. Prior to serving in the Senate, he represented Rhode Island in the U.S. House of Representatives from 1991 to 1997.
