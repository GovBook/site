Jacky Rosen is an American politician serving as the United States Senator from Nevada since 2019. A member of the Democratic Party, she previously represented Nevada's 3rd congressional district in the U.S. House of Representatives from 2017 to 2019. Born on August 2, 1957, Rosen has a background in software engineering and business.
