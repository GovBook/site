John A. Barrasso is an American physician and Republican politician serving as the senior United States Senator from Wyoming since 2007. A trained orthopedic surgeon, he has held leadership roles in the United States Senate, including the position of Senate Republican Conference Chair and, beginning in 2025, Senate Majority Whip.
