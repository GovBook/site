Kevin Cramer is an American politician serving as the U.S. Senator from North Dakota since 2019. A Republican, he previously represented North Dakota in the U.S. House of Representatives from 2013 to 2019. Before entering Congress, Cramer built a career in business and broadcasting, and he focuses on energy, agriculture, and rural issues.
