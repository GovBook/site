Lisa Blunt Rochester is the junior United States Senator from Delaware, serving since 2025. She previously represented Delaware in the U.S. House from 2017 to 2025. A member of the Democratic Party, her work focuses on healthcare, education, economic development, and rural and aging issues in Delaware.
