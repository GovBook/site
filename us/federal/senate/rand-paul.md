Rand Paul is an American physician and politician who has served as the junior United States Senator from Kentucky since 2011. A member of the Republican Party, he is the son of physician and former congressman Ron Paul. Paul is known for libertarian-leaning views on civil liberties, criminal justice reform, and limited government.
