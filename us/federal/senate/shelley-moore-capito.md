Shelley Moore Capito is the senior United States Senator from West Virginia, serving since 2015, and previously represented West Virginia in the U.S. House. A Republican, she focuses on infrastructure, energy, and health care policy, and is active in Senate committees related to Appropriations and Environment and Public Works.
